## Basic editing
https://code.visualstudio.com/docs/editing/codebasics

Alt+Click
    add secondary cursors

Ctrl+D 
    selects the word at the cursor

Shift+Alt 
    Column (box) selection while dragging to the opposite corner
    
Ctrl+Shift+F 
    enter search term over all files in the currently opened folder
    
Up, Down
    reuse a previous search term

\u and \l 
    upper/lowercase a single character
    
\U and \L 
    upper/lowercase the rest of the matching group

$n 
    n is the order of the capture group

Ctrl+Space
    manually trigger the IntelliSense suggestions

Ctrl+K Ctrl+0
    folds all regions in the editor
